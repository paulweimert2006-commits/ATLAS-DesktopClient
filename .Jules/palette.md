## 2026-02-26 - Consolidation of Expert Actions
**Learning:** In a production application with technical roots (BiPRO/SOAP), moving expert or secondary actions into a dedicated "More Options" menu (using `QMenu`) dramatically improves the "standard" user experience without losing power-user functionality.
**Action:** Always favor a single dominant primary action button and hide secondary variants behind a menu or "Advanced" toggle to reduce cognitive load.
