## 2026-03-08 - BiPRO UI Accessibility and Visual Recognition
**Learning:** Combining visual cues (emojis) with standard keyboard shortcuts (F5, Ctrl+R, etc.) significantly reduces cognitive load and improves accessibility for power users. When using shortcuts for destructive or expert actions (like Acknowledge All), they must be clearly communicated in tooltips and ideally use complex combinations (Ctrl+Alt+A) to prevent accidental triggers.
**Action:** Always append shortcut hints in parentheses to tooltips and use standard persona-linked emojis (👤, 🛠) for mode toggles.
